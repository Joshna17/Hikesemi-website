As a global provider of ultimate storage products and solutions, HIKSEMI is committed to continuous innovation to meet the diverse needs of applications including industrial control, video surveillance, data centers, consumer electronics, and more.

We leverage years of deep engineering expertise and state-of-the-art manufacturing to produce storage products that deliver exceptional value at every price point — from everyday USB drives to enterprise-class NVMe arrays.

Our product development philosophy centers on three principles: performance that sets benchmarks, reliability that customers can trust, and design that elevates the experience. Every HIKSEMI product is an expression of these values.

From our headquarters in Hangzhou's Binjiang District — China's 'Silicon Valley' — our team of engineers, designers, and support specialists work together to bring next-generation storage technology to users worldwide.
