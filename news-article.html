HIKSEMI Launches FUTURE CORE — World's Fastest Consumer SSD at 14,000 MB/s

The breakthrough PCIe 5.0 NVMe SSD arrives with a custom RGB heatsink, available in 1TB and 2TB — redefining what enthusiast storage can deliver.

FUTURE CORE
PCIe 5.0 NVMe SSD

HIKSEMI, the global storage brand of Hangzhou Hikstorage Technology Co., Ltd., today announced the commercial launch of the FUTURE CORE — its first PCIe 5.0 NVMe solid-state drive and the fastest consumer SSD the company has ever built. With sequential read speeds reaching 14,000 MB/s and write speeds of 12,000 MB/s, the FUTURE CORE resets performance expectations across the enthusiast PC market.

Breaking Every Speed Record in Consumer Storage

Built on the PCIe 5.0 x4 interface with NVMe 2.0 support, the FUTURE CORE leverages a custom-designed Gen 5 controller paired with Tier 1 3D NAND flash to achieve sustained throughput levels that were once reserved exclusively for enterprise-class drives. The result is a product that dramatically reduces load times, file transfer operations, and cold-boot sequences for even the most demanding workloads.

In benchmark testing across multiple independent reviewers, the FUTURE CORE achieved sequential read speeds of up to 14,000 MB/s and sequential write speeds of 12,000 MB/s — figures that represent approximately a 2× uplift over the fastest PCIe 4.0 drives on the market today. For real-world tasks such as game loading, 4K video editing timelines, and large archive transfers, the difference is immediately and viscerally felt.

Engineering the Thermal Challenge

Operating a PCIe 5.0 NVMe SSD at peak throughput generates substantially more heat than any Gen 4 drive. HIKSEMI's engineering team addressed this head-on with a custom-designed aluminum heatsink that integrates ARGB lighting across 16 individually addressable zones. The heatsink is not purely cosmetic — its fin array is precisely calculated to maintain the controller junction temperature below the throttle threshold even under sustained 100% write workloads at ambient temperatures of up to 50°C.

The thermal solution is compatible with ASUS Aura Sync, MSI Mystic Light, Gigabyte RGB Fusion, and ASRock Polychrome Sync, allowing users to integrate the drive into their existing RGB ecosystem without additional software or proprietary controllers.

Platform Compatibility and PS5 Support

The FUTURE CORE is fully compatible with Intel's 12th, 13th, and 14th Generation Core platforms, AMD's Ryzen 7000 and Ryzen 9000 series on AM5, as well as the latest HEDT and workstation chipsets. HIKSEMI has also confirmed compatibility with the PlayStation 5 when installed with the included heatsink, making the FUTURE CORE a compelling upgrade path for console enthusiasts who want to expand their SSD storage beyond the built-in 825 GB drive.

For developers and creators working in DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, or Unreal Engine, the FUTURE CORE's high IOPS figures translate directly into faster project load times, snappier proxy generation, and reduced stutter during multi-stream 6K and 8K preview playback.

Pricing and Global Availability

The HIKSEMI FUTURE CORE is available immediately through HIKSEMI's authorized distribution network across 50+ countries. The 1TB model carries an MSRP of $149.99, while the 2TB variant is priced at $279.99. Both models include the ARGB heatsink in the box and ship with a 5-year warranty backed by HIKSEMI's global support infrastructure.

Regional distributors across North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East have received initial inventory. HIKSEMI has confirmed that additional stock allocation will follow in Q2 2025 to meet anticipated high demand. Prospective buyers are encouraged to contact their nearest authorized dealer or visit the HIKSEMI where-to-buy page for stocking information in their region.

Where to Buy

The FUTURE CORE is available through HIKSEMI's global partner network. Use the store locator at hiksemi.com/where-to-buy to find an authorized dealer near you, or contact the HIKSEMI enterprise sales team directly for volume and OEM enquiries.
